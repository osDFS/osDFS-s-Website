Our Mission

We are Open Source Digital Freedom Society. We are an off campus community that supports women & men that are interested in computer and information science and all tech things. We host tech talks, community events, and business leaders from all around the area on a weekly basis. Our goal is to create and inspire a strong community of women & men in technology fields. We aim to bring support, networking and mentoring to help women & men on your campus to find success in the classroom and in the office.
